About Us



Discovery


Founded in 2015, GlowStation LLC is an American cosmetics company founded by Information Technology experts Francisco Parayno and Arnemie Gayyed. The company began selling handcrafted wooden personal care accessories such as bamboo scrubs, body brushes that were set on January 25, 2015.

Beginning of company


In 2015, GlowStation started out as a small Hawaii-based business selling vegan beauty accessories such as wooden toothbrushes and bamboo body brushes. With instagram as its only platform of doing online business and having only 15,000 followers, success came in really slow. The owners, Nemie and Francisco, planned for the creation of a physical store that would be in the middle of Honolulu to attract customer attention. Little by little, they saved up money for their dream store. However, having a store put in the middle of the city was a challenge the duo could not handle. As a second plan, they had the store built near Waikiki beach instead. Little did they know that having their store in that location would be the beginning of their big-time success.

Company growth


The very first GlowStation store, GlowStation Honolulu, was the branch that got the most attention from customers, non-customers, and basically everyone who passed by it. This was because of its eye catching design, all made of wood and glass that really had a beach vibe while complimenting its surroundings. It became a stepping stone for the owners, a foundation for a new type of success. After all the attention the store was getting, Nemie thought it was not enough to just get peopleâ€™s attention. So she and Francisco decided to release more products in the coming year. After planning things out, the partners in business went with their final decision to produce skin care products. Though the process cost them dearly, all efforts were not put to waste as 2018 came and GlowStation received its very own brand approval from the Food and Drug Administration. The products were then starting to sell and production was starting to become a problem as the demand for quantity of items was getting too high. But this demand did not become a problem as the business partners saw this as an opportunity to improve. 2019 came and the GlowStation instagram page had over 50, 000 followers and was very happy to announce that they are opening a branch in Hilo as an extension to the main branch. What was most surprising for the owners was the fact that GlowStation was not just a store anymore but a tourist spot. With that said, it also hit Top 2 on the best beauty brand in Hawaii with only just one branch. As plenty of opportunities awaited GlowStation in other US states, more employees were hired to manage different branches all over Hawaii and the owners moved to New York. In the same year, the GlowStation website became available for ordering overseas while hundreds were then signing up to become brand ambassadors. By the time GlowStation was already available in California, Texas, Washington, and Florida, the brand was already stable.

About Owners


As graduates of the University of the Cordilleras(Baguio, Philippines), Arnemie Gayyed and Francisco Parayno were the two sole reasons for the creation of GlowStation. Hiring the correct employees to work with them, they were able to produce the quality they desired for their products. As Information Technology experts, they were able to incorporate technology into beauty by using the social media platforms that they had. With that platform, they then moved to creating their own to display their products online and show the world the hard work they have done.
